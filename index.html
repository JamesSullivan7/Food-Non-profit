Source notes

  • Tulsa Library's "Tulsa Area Food Resources" page was updated November 14, 2025 and provided most pantry, meal, hours, and eligibility details.
  • Food Bank of Eastern Oklahoma pages were used for the regional pantry finder, emergency box help, and Community Food Bank contact details.
  • Cause IQ's Tulsa food distribution directory was used to make sure major Tulsa-area food organizations such as Community Food Bank of Eastern Oklahoma, Meals on Wheels of Metro Tulsa, Iron Gate Tulsa, Food On The Move, and Hunger Free Oklahoma were represented.
  • Direct program sites were used when they gave clearer current service descriptions or addresses than directory pages.
